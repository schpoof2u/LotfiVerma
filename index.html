What subjects do we plan to teach?

  • English Grammar

  • Pre-Algebra
  • Algebra 1
  • Environmental Science
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

What is the purpose of this site?

  • To educate anyone, including non-English speakers
  • Specifically towards low-income students who have a passion for learning but can't afford it
  • To create a space where students can access the best materials for learning not to need to go on a wild goose chase to get valid and trustworthy knowledge
  • Prepare students for college-rigor and introduce them to adult life (How to manage your money and how to make passive income which includes investing
  • To include different learning methods, language translations, auditory, videos,
    articles, and a bunch of learning techniques that make the student comfortable with the way they learn and eliminate the need to confirm which harms their learning capability.
